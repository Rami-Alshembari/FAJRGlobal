Data source & methodology

This platform aggregates data from FAJR Global's field reporting workbooks. Hospital data is parsed directly from the Updated Report Excel files. Voucher data is extracted from weekly purchase records. Project data is sourced from the Projects portfolio workbook. Dashboards operate as static browser-based analytics to support offline and low-bandwidth environments.

Interpretation notes

Budget figures shown for the projects portfolio may be incomplete where values were marked "under review" in source data. Beneficiary counts reflect reported reach as recorded in field documentation. Voucher quantity totals reflect aggregated purchase records and may differ slightly from original register totals due to rounding.
